Welcome to Al Razack Tours Internationals

Haj & Umrah tour organisers

After successfully organising Haj Pilgrimage Services To Hajis by Ship Through Haj Committee, Bombay AL HAJ ABDUL RAZACK SAB started AL RAZACK TOURS INTERNATIONAL in the year 1997 at Bangalore (Tumkur).
